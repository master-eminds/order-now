The Order Now App is specially designed to automate your order management. It helps your waiters to take their orders precisely and convey them to the kitchen so that all your dine-in and takeaway orders get properly organised.

How to use our app

Use QR Scan button at the bottom of the application in order to scan the QR code and preview the menu for your favourite restaurant.

Social Login is single sign-on for end users. Using existing login information from a social network provider like Facebook or Google, the user can sign into a third party application instead of creating a new account specifically for that application. This simplifies registrations and logins for end users. You can also authenticate via custom email adress or you can still
